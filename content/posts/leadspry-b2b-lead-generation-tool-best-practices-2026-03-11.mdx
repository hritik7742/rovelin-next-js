## 5. Follow Legal Guidelines for Data Compliance

Scraping contact info isn’t illegal, but it must be done responsibly. The FTC’s CAN-SPAM Act and GDPR require:

- Opt-out options in marketing emails  
- Accurate sender information  
- No deceptive headers or subject lines  

LeadSpry helps by avoiding the extraction of private or protected data (e.g., non-public LinkedIn emails). Still, ensure you:

- **Verify opt-in status** where possible.  
- **Respect website "robots.txt" rules** (most tools handle this automatically).  
- **Document your data sources** for audits.
