## Step 2: Creating and Managing Custom Prompts

Custom prompts are the cornerstone of DeepSeek Pro’s productivity suite. They allow you to automate repetitive tasks and ensure consistency across projects.

### How to Create a Custom Prompt

1. Open a DeepSeek AI chat session.
2. Click the **Custom Prompts** button (represented by a clipboard icon).
3. Select **Create New Prompt**.
4. Enter your instruction, such as:
   - *“Generate a weekly meeting summary based on the following notes...”*
   - *“Draft an email response to a customer complaint about shipping delays.”*
5. Assign a name and optional tags (e.g., *Project Management*, *Customer Support*).
6. Save the prompt to your default folder or a specific project folder.

### Use Cases for Custom Prompts

- **Content Creation:** Save templates for blog outlines, social media posts, or SEO meta descriptions.
- **Programming:** Store snippets for common coding questions or debugging workflows.
- **Customer Support:** Preload responses for frequently asked questions or troubleshooting guides.

By reusing these prompts, you eliminate the need to rewrite the same instructions repeatedly, saving time and reducing errors.

## Step 3: Organizing Chats with Folders and Themes

With DeepSeek Pro, you can categorize your AI interactions using folders and personalize the interface with themes to suit your workflow.

### Folder Management

1. Click the **Folders** panel on the left sidebar.
2. Create a new folder (e.g., *Marketing Campaigns*, *Client A*, *Personal Projects*).
3. Drag and drop existing chats into folders or assign new chats to a folder during creation.
4. Use nested folders for multi-layered organization.

**Example:** If you’re managing a product launch, create a folder named *Product X Launch*. Subfolders could include *Market Research*, *Copywriting*, and *Analytics Reports*.

### Custom Themes for Better Focus

1. Go to **Settings > Themes**.
2. Choose from pre-designed themes (e.g., Dark Mode, High Contrast) or create a custom one.
3. Adjust font sizes, background colors, and button styles for readability.
4. Save your theme and apply it to specific folders or globally.

**Why It Matters:** Visual customization reduces cognitive load. For instance, switching to a minimalist theme during deep work sessions can minimize distractions.

## Step 4: Leveraging Voice Input for Hands-Free Efficiency

Voice input is particularly handy for users who need to input instructions while multitasking—think writers brainstorming on a tablet or customer service reps transcribing tickets.

### How to Use Voice Input

1. Ensure your browser permits microphone access.
2. Open a DeepSeek AI chat and click the **Voice Input** button (microphone icon).
3. Say your prompt aloud (e.g., *“Compare the market share of Tesla and BYD in 2025”).
4. DeepSeek Pro converts speech to text and sends the prompt automatically.

**Best Practices:**
- Use a quiet environment for accurate transcription.
- Keep sentences concise to avoid overlapping audio processing.

**Pro Tip:** Pair voice input with custom prompts for maximum efficiency. For example, say *“[Customer Support] Generate refund policy response”* to trigger a pre-saved prompt.

## Step 5: Exporting and Archiving Chats

DeepSeek Pro’s chat export feature ensures you never lose valuable AI-generated content. You can export conversations in multiple formats for documentation, sharing, or offline analysis.

### Exporting a Chat

1. Open the chat you want to export.
2. Click the **Export** button (three dots menu) and select **Export as Text/Markdown/PDF**.
3. Choose a destination folder on your device.
4. Review the exported file to verify all messages are included.

### Use Cases for Chat Export

- **Documentation:** Export meetings notes or brainstorming sessions for future reference.
- **Team Sharing:** Share a PDF of a collaborative AI project with non-technical stakeholders.
- **Backup:** Regularly export critical chats to cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox).

**Example:** A marketing manager exports a chat containing competitor analysis results to include in a quarterly report.

Enable Chrome’s extension sync feature to access your custom prompts, folders, and themes on all your devices seamlessly.
