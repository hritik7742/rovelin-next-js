## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  

**H2: How do I get started with Blur Screen?**  
Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store and click the icon to start blurring. Use the mouse to draw blur zones and adjust opacity as needed.  

**H2: Can I use Blur Screen on multiple devices?**  
Yes, the extension works across any Chrome browser, but settings aren’t synced automatically. Reapply blurs per device.  

**H2: Is Blur Screen secure?**  
The tool doesn’t store or transmit your data. All blurs are applied locally to your browser, ensuring zero exposure risks.  

**H2: Does Blur Screen interfere with screen sharing?**  
Not at all. The blurs are visible to all participants in a screen-sharing session, just like the rest of your screen.
