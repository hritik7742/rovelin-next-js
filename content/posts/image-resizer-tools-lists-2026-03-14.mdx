## 5 Common Mistakes to Avoid When Resizing Images

Even with the best tools, mistakes happen. Avoid these pitfalls:

1. **Ignoring Aspect Ratios:** Stretching an image to fit a box creates distortion. Always lock proportions unless intentional.
2. **Over-Compressing:** Aggressive JPEG compression introduces artifacts. Test different quality levels.
3. **Using the Wrong Tool for the Job:** A basic photo resizer won’t handle SVG graphics or layered PSD files.
4. **Neglecting Resolution for Print:** Print projects require 300 DPI—many tools default to 72 DPI for web.
5. **Relying on Automatic Presets Blindly:** Platform recommendations may not align with your design goals (e.g., oversizing a logo for impact).
