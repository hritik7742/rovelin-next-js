## Step-by-Step Guide to PDF to JPG Conversion

### 1. Preparing Your PDF File

Before initiating conversion, optimize your source document for the best results:
- **Simplify content**: Remove unnecessary pages or layers to reduce file size and improve processing speed.
- **Standardize formatting**: Ensure fonts and images are properly embedded to avoid rendering errors.
- **Password removal**: If your PDF is secured, decrypt it first (using tools like PDF Unlocker) to avoid conversion errors.

### 2. Uploading to a Browser-Based Converter

Upload your PDF using these best practices:
- **Drag-and-drop interface**: Most modern tools support this for faster file transfer.
- **Page range selection**: Extract specific pages instead of converting entire documents (ideal for legal contracts or research papers).
- **Preview functionality**: Check page layout before conversion to identify potential alignment issues.
