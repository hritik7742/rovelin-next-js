## Why Convert PNG to JPG in the First Place?

Before diving into tool comparisons, let’s address the core use case: **why convert PNG files to JPG?** The answer lies in three key advantages:

1. **Smaller File Sizes**: JPG compression reduces file size by up to 60% compared to PNG, making JPEG ideal for platforms with strict size limits.
2. **Wider Compatibility**: JPG remains the standard format for most websites, email clients, and legacy systems that struggle with PNG transparency.
3. **No Transparency Required**: JPG handles solid-color backgrounds better than PNG when transparency is unnecessary or unwanted.

However, converting PNG to JPG isn’t without drawbacks. Transparency is permanently removed, and JPG’s lossy compression can degrade image quality if overused. This is where the choice of tool becomes critical.
