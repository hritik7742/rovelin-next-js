## Setting Up Your Productivity-Boosting Screensaver

Configuring **Custom Screensaver - HD Videos & Images** is straightforward. Follow these steps to integrate it into your workflow:

### Step 1: Install the Extension
   - Visit the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/jkegjaomefbejnfmahjfjidfelncgpma) and add the extension to your browser. It works across devices and requires no additional software.

### Step 2: Choose Your Media
   - **Upload HD Images**: Select high-quality photos from your computer (JPEG, PNG, or HEIC formats).
   - **Upload Videos**: Use MP4 or WebM files for dynamic content (e.g., looping animations or short clips).
   - **External Links**: Point to URLs for cloud-hosted media (e.g., Google Drive or Dropbox links).

### Step 3: Set Idle Time Thresholds
   - Adjust the inactivity time before the screensaver activates (e.g., 5 to 30 minutes).
   - Enable “Dim Screen” or “Blackout Mode” if you prefer minimal distractions during breaks.

### Step 4: Customize Playback
   - **Shuffle Media**: Randomize order for variety.
   - **Loop Videos**: Set specific durations or endless playback.
   - **Sound Options**: Mute by default or enable ambient audio for immersive sessions.

### FAQ

#### **1. Can I use this with browsers other than Chrome?**
The extension is designed for Chrome, but similar tools exist for Firefox and Edge. For cross-browser compatibility, ensure your browser supports Chrome extensions.

#### **2. What video formats are supported?**
MP4 and WebM files work best. Ensure videos are under 2GB and encoded with H.264 for smooth playback.

#### **3. Does the screensaver work on multiple monitors?**
Yes, but the extension activates independently on each browser instance. For multi-monitor setups, configure settings per device.

#### **4. How does it handle automatic screen locking?**
The extension activates before the system locks screen. Adjust idle time thresholds to avoid conflicts.

#### **5. Can I schedule different media for different times of day?**
Currently, the extension doesn’t support time-based playlists. Use separate profiles or scripts for advanced scheduling.

#### **6. Is there a mobile version available?**
The tool is browser-based, so it works on mobile Chrome. However, touch interactions and screen size may limit video playback.
